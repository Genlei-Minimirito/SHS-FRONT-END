About Genlei's Career

Genlei Minimirito also known as Deemo/Touji/Aphrodite was a former amateur player in Mobile Legends:Bang Bang(MLBB). He was an amateur player for two and a half years, and played for the team Trinity Esports and Fenrir Esports. But when the face to face classes came back, he stopped playing and he focused on his studies. He was a also taking art commission like digital art and graphic designs for influencer's pages. Taking commissions help him out to gain extra income as he leave the tournament life. As of now he's a senior highscool student and taking the TVL ICT strand in Laguna State Polytechnic University in San Pablo.
